## Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the **DpadRecyclerView** open-source library and sample, maintained and available on GitHub.

### 1. Introduction

The **DpadRecyclerView sample** showcases the integration of `DpadRecyclerView`, a component designed to enhance focus navigation in `RecyclerView` for D-pad/remote-controlled devices (like Android TV). **It is a client-side library and does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information or usage data.**

### 2. Data Collection and Use

* **No Personal Data is Collected:** The Library does not have any functionality to collect, transmit, or store personal data from users of applications that integrate it.
* **No Tracking:** The Library does not use cookies, tracking technologies, or analytics services.
* **No External Communication:** The Library does not communicate with any external servers or services.
